My Favorite Movies

The Dark Knight

This movie is one of my favorite Superhero movies because it is
grounded in the dark reality and the philosophy of the order and justice.

This movie is about the Batman fighting the menacing Joker from destroying
his city, Gotham. The Joker will do everything he can to destroy everything
that Batman holds dear and Batman must save Gotham and its citizens.

This movie is the best superhero movie that DC can offer in lifetime.

Tazza: The High Rollers

Tazza is a Korean gambling film where the main character, Goni,
trains to become one of the best swindler using Korea's famous card game,
Hwatu.

I consider this movie one of the best movie that Korea has to offer
without being too graphic or too grainy. This movie is realistic, and
at the same time, keeps entertaining audiences with poetic storyline.

Kimi No Na Wa

Kimi No Na Wa is a Japanese Animation film that revolves around the
two high schoolers, Mitsuha and Taki. They soon learn that they switched
their bodies and begins to learn about each other despite being in two
different regions.

I like this movie because it keeps the romance, mystery, and action at
entertaining pace. The ending that this movie provides is satisfying
comedy and the main characters each serve their purposes without
being too dull.
